Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for annual plans.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. For annual plans, you'll retain access until the end of your billing period.

Is there a free trial available?

We offer a 7-day free trial for the Professional plan so you can test all features before committing.

How does the video creation limit work?

Each generated video counts as one creation. Unused creations don't roll over to the next month.

What happens if I exceed my monthly limit?

You'll have the option to purchase additional credits or upgrade your plan if you need more creations.

Do you offer discounts for non-profits?

Yes, we offer 30% discounts for registered non-profit organizations. Contact our team for verification.
